Maria Vincent was a French film actress and singer.


== Biography ==

Maria Vincent was born on 23 October 1929 in Marseille, Bouches-du-Rhône, France. She was married to Léo Marciano. She was known for her roles in the films Interpol Against X (1960), Secret of the Chinese Carnation (1964), and Hotel Clausewitz (1967).Vincent died in Paris on 28 August 2006. She's the grandmother of Paola Dicelli, journalist in Elle Magazine France.
