Boris Sergeyevich Brunov (Russian: Бори́с Серге́евич Бруно́в; 10 June 1922, Tiflis – 2 September 1997, Moscow) was a Soviet and Russian actor and entertainer. In 1985, he was named a People's Artist of the RSFSR. He was a director of the Moscow theatre Estrada.
