== Early life and career ==
Doyle was born in Lincoln, Nebraska, and was the younger sister of actor David Doyle. She acted in productions of the Lincoln Community Playhouse and graduated from the Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre.Doyle's television appearances included The Philco Television Playhouse. She also starred in the 1976 Broadway play Best Friend. Her other work on Broadway included performing in The Glass Menagerie (1983), Clothes for a Summer Hotel (1980), Best Friend (1976), Equus (1974), and King Henry V (1969).


== Personal life and death ==
Doyle was married to producer John A. McQiggan. She died of lung cancer in New York City, at the age of 63.
