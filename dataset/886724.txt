Dimitris Papamichael (Greek: Δημήτρης Παπαμιχαήλ Greek pronunciation: [ðiˈmitɾis papamixaˈil]; 1934–2004) was a popular Greek actor and director. In 1965, he married Aliki Vougiouklaki, the so-called "national star" of Greece. The star couple remained married for a decade, co-starring in several films that marked the "golden era" of Greek cinema.
