He is perhaps best known for his long association with Sonny Rollins. Cranshaw performed in Rollins's working band on and off for over five decades, starting with a live appearance at the 1959 Playboy jazz festival in Chicago and on record with the 1962 album The Bridge.Cranshaw died at the age of 83 on November 2, 2016, in Manhattan, New York, from Stage IV cancer.
