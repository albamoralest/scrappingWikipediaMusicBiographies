Georgy Ivanovich Drozd (Ukrainian: Георгій Іванович Дрозд; 28 May 1941, Kyiv –  10 June 2015, Kyiv) was a Soviet and Ukrainian actor, member of the National Union of Cinematographers of Ukraine. People's Artist of Ukraine (1999).


== Biography ==
Born May 28, 1941 in Kyiv in the family working. He graduated from the Kyiv National I. K. Karpenko-Kary Theatre, Cinema and Television University.
He worked at the Lesya Ukrainka National Academic Theater of Russian Drama.
His son is actor Maxim Drozd.


== Death ==
In 2013 the actor became seriously ill. June 10, 2015 Georgy Drozd died after a long illness.
