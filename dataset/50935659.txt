Elena Nikolayevna Gogoleva (7 April 1900 [O.S. 25 March 1900] Moscow â€“ 15 November 1993) was a Soviet and Russian film and stage actress, actress of the Maly Theatre in Moscow from 1918 to 1993. She was a People's Artist of the USSR (1949), Hero of Socialist Labour (1974), and the winner of three Stalin Prizes (1947, 1948, 1949). She joined the CPSU in 1948.
